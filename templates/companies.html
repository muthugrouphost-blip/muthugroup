Packaging Films

Muthu Food Films

"Packaging the Future of Food."

About the Company

Muthu Food Films is the flagship manufacturing company of the Muthu Groups, specializing in high-performance polyethylene and laminated films for the food and dairy industry. With state-of-the-art extrusion and lamination equipment, we produce films that meet the stringent quality demands of dairy cooperatives, oil producers, and food processors.

Our production facility is equipped with modern quality control systems to ensure every roll that leaves our plant is consistent, reliable, and fit for purpose.

Flexible Packaging

Muthu Shri Chakra Packaging Pvt. Ltd.

"Wrapping Quality Into Every Layer."

About the Company

Muthu Shri Chakra Packaging Private Limited is a dedicated manufacturer of flexible packaging products, serving clients who demand both functional performance and branding excellence. Our production capabilities include multi-layer lamination, tarpaulin weaving, and multi-color printing.

We take pride in delivering products that are consistent, vibrant, and built for demanding environments — catering to the complete packaging needs of dairy brands, oil companies, and industrial clients.

Industrial Tubing

Muthu Multi Formation

"Industrial Strength. Built to Flow."

About the Company

Muthu Multi Formation is the industrial arm of Muthu Groups, focused on the manufacture of PVC flexible tubes and hoses for agricultural, hydraulic, and general industrial applications. Our products are engineered for long service life and reliable performance under challenging conditions.

We serve a wide range of clients across agriculture, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors, providing consistent tubing solutions backed by thorough quality inspection at every production stage.
