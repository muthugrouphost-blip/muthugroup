Muthu Food Films — Product Range

Muthu Food Films specializes in the manufacture of high-quality polyethylene films designed for the food and dairy sector. Our films are engineered to ensure food safety, product freshness, and reliable performance across a variety of packaging applications.

Polyethylene (PE) Films for Milk Packaging

Manufactured using premium-grade LDPE resin, our PE films for milk packaging offer excellent tensile strength, leak resistance, and clarity — ensuring dairy products reach consumers in perfect condition. Suitable for both pouched and bulk milk packaging formats.

  • High seal strength
  • Food-grade material
  • Leak-proof construction
  • 50–200 microns
  • Printable surface
  • Excellent clarity & gloss

Polyethylene Films for Edible Oil Packaging

Specifically formulated for edible oil applications, these films provide a superior moisture and oxygen barrier, preventing contamination and preserving quality during storage and transportation. Conforms to food safety regulations.

  • High barrier properties
  • Puncture resistant
  • Grease & oil resistant
  • Custom widths & thicknesses
  • Automated packaging compatible

Shrink Films

Designed for tamper-evident and multi-pack bundling applications. When heat is applied, the film conforms tightly to the product shape, offering a clean, professional appearance with strong holding force. Widely used in beverage, dairy, and FMCG industries.

  • Shrink ratio up to 75%
  • Crystal-clear transparency
  • Strong sealing edges
  • Resistant to tearing
  • Center-fold & single-wound rolls

Laminated Films

Our laminated films combine multiple substrate layers to deliver enhanced mechanical strength, barrier protection, and printability. Ideal for packaging applications that require both protection and visual branding, particularly in food and consumer goods sectors.

  • PE/BOPP/Nylon combinations
  • Moisture & oxygen barrier
  • Excellent print adhesion
  • High-clarity finish
  • Custom roll sizes

Muthu Shri Chakra Packaging — Product Range

Muthu Shri Chakra Packaging Private Limited is a trusted manufacturer of flexible packaging solutions. Our production capabilities span laminated films, heavy-duty tarpaulins, and printed poly films, all manufactured to meet the specific requirements of our clients in the dairy, oil, and industrial sectors.

Laminated Films

Multi-layer laminated films that offer exceptional strength, barrier properties, and a superior finish. Widely used for packaging milk, oil, snacks, and other food products. Our lamination process ensures excellent bond strength and uniformity across every roll.

  • PE/BOPP/Foil combinations
  • High bond strength
  • Moisture & grease barrier
  • High-speed machine compatible
  • Custom widths & print-ready

Tarpaulins

Heavy-duty, woven polyethylene-based covers designed for outdoor protection, agriculture, construction, and industrial storage. Manufactured for maximum durability and weather resistance — available in a range of sizes, thicknesses, and colors as per client requirements.

  • UV stabilized
  • Waterproof & tear-resistant
  • Reinforced edges & eyelets
  • Multiple GSM options
  • Customizable size & color

Designed for edible oil brands requiring functional and attractive packaging — combining barrier performance with excellent print quality. Produced under hygienic conditions, conforming to food safety standards.

  • Grease & oil barrier

Muthu Multi Formation — Product Range

Muthu Multi Formation is a specialist manufacturer of PVC-based flexible tubes and hoses for industrial applications. Our products are built to withstand demanding conditions, offering flexibility, durability, and consistent performance in a variety of industrial environments.

PVC Flexible Tubes

Manufactured from high-quality, virgin-grade PVC compound, offering excellent flexibility across a wide temperature range. Used in agricultural irrigation, water supply systems, laboratory setups, and general industrial fluid transfer applications.

  • Non-toxic & food-safe options
  • -5°C to +60°C range
  • Clear and opaque variants
  • 1/4" to 4" inner diameter
  • UV-resistant options
  • High burst pressure rating

PVC Braided Hose

Reinforced with high-strength polyester braid for medium to high-pressure fluid and air applications. The braided reinforcement prevents kinking and enhances pressure-handling capacity — ideal for compressed air lines, hydraulic systems, and water delivery.

  • High-tensile polyester braid
  • Excellent kink resistance
  • Up to 20 bar operating pressure
  • Smooth bore for unrestricted flow
  • Transparent & colored variants
  • Sizes: 1/4" to 2"

LD Flat Hose (Low-Density Polyethylene Flat Hose)

Manufactured from low-density polyethylene, designed for agricultural and irrigation applications. Flattens compactly for easy storage — when water flows through, it inflates to full round cross-section for efficient delivery.

  • Lightweight & compact when flat
  • Easy to roll & store
  • Drip & sprinkler irrigation
  • Chemical & fertilizer resistant
  • Various bore sizes & lengths
  • High-flow capacity
