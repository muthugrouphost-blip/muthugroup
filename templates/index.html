Who We Are

About Muthu Groups

Muthu Groups is a South India-based industrial conglomerate with decades of experience in manufacturing high-quality packaging films and industrial tubing products. Founded on the principles of reliability, precision, and customer satisfaction, the group has grown into a trusted name serving dairy, food processing, oil, and industrial sectors across the region.

With three specialized companies operating under its umbrella, Muthu Groups delivers end-to-end packaging and tubing solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and durability.
