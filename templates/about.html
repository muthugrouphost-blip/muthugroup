Our Journey

Our Story

Muthu Groups was established with a single vision — to deliver world-class manufacturing solutions to industries that depend on quality materials every day. What began as a focused packaging film manufacturing unit has today grown into a diversified group of three specialized companies, each serving distinct but complementary sectors of the packaging and industrial tubing market.

Headquartered in Tamil Nadu, India, the group has built strong, lasting relationships with clients across the dairy, food, oil, and industrial sectors. Every product that leaves our facilities carries our commitment to precision, consistency, and performance.
